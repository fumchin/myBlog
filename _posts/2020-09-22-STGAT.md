## Introduction
>S - Spatial  
>T - Temporal  
>GAT - Graph Attention Network（GAN已經有人用了）

* **Spatial interactions** captured by the graph attention mechanism at each time step 
  * M-LSTM & GAT
* Extra LSTM to encode the **temporal correlations** of interaction 
  * G-LSTM
